About Me

From a very young age I was interested in cryptography. In Elementary I was introduced to basic letter swap ciphers, in Middle School I got a book on symbology, cryptography and numerology, in High School I started to learn to code. It took till college for me to really glue together those disparate parts intro an understanding of Network Engineering and Cybersecurity.

I am a 22 year old currently attending Texas A&M University with a Major in Electronic Systems and Engineering Technology and a minor in Cybersecurity. One of my main areas of interest currently is in the function and security of Software Defined Networks as well as integrating embedded systems into a relatively disparate network. I also work as an on-campus AV Troubleshooter/Helpdesk ensuring that operation of classes goes smoothly and can recover quickly from technial malfunction.
